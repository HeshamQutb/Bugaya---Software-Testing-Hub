üí° Career Tips

Start with Manual Testing

Build a strong foundation in manual testing before moving to automation. Understanding the "why" behind testing is crucial.

Practice with Real Projects

Create a portfolio by testing open-source applications or building your own test projects to showcase your skills.

Stay Updated

The testing field evolves rapidly. Follow testing blogs, join communities, and continuously learn new tools and techniques.

Specialize Gradually

Once you master the basics, choose a specialization (UI, API, Mobile, Performance) based on your interests and market demand.
